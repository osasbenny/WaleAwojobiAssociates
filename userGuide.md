## Using Your Website

### Homepage Navigation

The homepage introduces visitors to Wale Awojobi Associates with a striking hero section featuring your agency tagline and a property search interface.

**Hero Section:** Click "View All Listings" to explore the complete property catalog, or use the search filters to narrow down by property type, region, and price range right on the homepage.

**Featured Properties:** Browse four premium property picks displayed as cards showing images, prices, location, and key details like bedrooms and bathrooms. Click "View Details" on any property to learn more.

**Company Statistics:** See impressive numbers highlighting 100+ happy clients, 50+ five-star reviews, 200+ successful sales, and 10+ years of experience.

**Testimonials:** Scroll through client success stories using the carousel navigation arrows to read feedback from satisfied customers.

### Navigation Menu

The sticky navigation bar at the top provides quick access to all sections:

- **Home** → Returns to the homepage
- **About Us** → Learn about the agency's mission, values, and services
- **Listings** → Access the full property catalog with advanced filters
- **Our Team** → Meet the experienced professionals behind the agency
- **Blog** → Read articles about real estate tips and market insights
- **Contact Us** → Send inquiries or schedule consultations

### Property Listings Page

The dedicated listings page offers comprehensive property search and filtering:

**Filter Properties:** Use the filter panel to search by property type (Apartments, Houses, Land, Offices, Industrial), region (Abuja, Lagos, Port Harcourt, Kano, Ibadan), price range (NGN 0-1M through 20M+), and status (For Sale or For Rent).

**View Results:** Properties display as cards with images, titles, locations, prices, and property details. The results counter shows how many properties match your filters.

**Property Details:** Each card shows bedrooms, bathrooms, and square footage. Click "View Details" to see the full property information.

**Compare Properties:** Click "Compare" on any property to add it to your comparison list.

### About Us Page

Discover the agency's background and expertise:

**Mission Statement:** Understand Wale Awojobi Associates' commitment to providing exceptional estate surveying and valuation services.

**Core Values:** Learn about the agency's dedication to professionalism, integrity, expertise, and client focus.

**Services Offered:** Explore property valuation, estate surveying, property management, and project appraisal services.

**Why Choose Us:** Review key differentiators including 10+ years of experience, certified professionals, comprehensive market knowledge, and competitive pricing.

### Team Page

Meet the professionals:

**Team Members:** View cards for each team member displaying their name, title (Principal Partner, Estate Surveyor, Accountant, ICT Consultant), credentials, and professional biography.

**Expertise:** Each profile highlights the individual's specialization and contribution to client success.

### Blog Section

Stay informed with real estate insights:

**Article List:** Browse articles organized by category (Valuation, Investment, Services) with publication dates and author names.

**Article Preview:** Each article shows a thumbnail, title, excerpt, and author information. Click "Read More" to view the full article.

**Categories:** Filter articles by topic to find content most relevant to your interests.

### Contact Page

Connect with the agency:

**Contact Form:** Fill in your full name, email address, reason for contact (Property Inquiry, Valuation Services, Surveying Services, Property Management, or Other), and your message. Click "Send Message" to submit.

**Contact Details:** Find phone numbers (07788249154, 08078697158), email address (waleawojobi@gmail.com), and physical address (58 Accra Street, Zone 5, Wuse Abuja, Nigeria).

**WhatsApp Chat:** Click the green WhatsApp button in the bottom-right corner to start an instant conversation on WhatsApp.

## Managing Your Website

### Using the Management UI

Access the Management UI panel from the chatbox header to manage your website:

**Preview Panel:** View a live preview of your website as visitors see it. Test all pages and interactive features.

**Code Panel:** Download all project files for backup or local development. The file tree shows your complete codebase structure.

**Dashboard Panel:** Monitor website analytics, view visitor statistics, and track page performance metrics.

**Database Panel:** Manage any data stored for your website (contact form submissions, property listings, etc.). Full connection details are available in Settings.

**Settings Panel:** Customize your website name, logo, domain, and other configuration options. Update environment secrets in the Secrets sub-panel.

### Customizing Content

**Update Company Information:** Edit the company name, phone numbers, email, and address in the project constants file.

**Add Properties:** Modify the mock properties data in the constants file to add new listings with images, prices, descriptions, and details.

**Update Team Members:** Change team member names, titles, credentials, and biographies in the team data.

**Add Blog Articles:** Create new blog posts by adding entries to the blog articles data with title, excerpt, category, and publication date.

**Modify Testimonials:** Update client testimonials with new feedback and success stories.

### Deployment

When you are ready to publish your website:

1. Click the **"Publish"** button in the Management UI header
2. Your website will be deployed to a public URL
3. Share the URL with clients and partners
4. Monitor analytics in the Dashboard panel to track visitor engagement
